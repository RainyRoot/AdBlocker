Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-22

Summary

AdBlocker does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Data collection

AdBlocker collects no data whatsoever. Specifically:

  • No browsing history is recorded or transmitted.
  • No analytics, telemetry, or crash reports are sent.
  • No personally identifiable information is processed.

Filter lists

Filter rules (EasyList, EasyPrivacy, uBlock filters) are downloaded once at build time and bundled into the extension. The extension never fetches remote URLs at runtime; no network requests are made on your behalf.

Local storage

The extension stores the following data locally on your device using chrome.storage.local:

  • Whether the extension is enabled (on/off toggle).
  • Your personal allowlist of domains where blocking is paused.
  • A running count of blocked requests (used for the stats display).
  • Any custom blocking rules you have entered.

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to any third party.

Permissions

AdBlocker requests only the permissions strictly required to block ads: declarativeNetRequest, storage, tabs, and scripting. No permission is used to observe, record, or transmit your activity.
